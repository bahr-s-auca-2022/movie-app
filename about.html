About Movie App

Welcome to the Movie App! This application is designed to bring the world of cinema to your fingertips. Discover trending movies, explore detailed information including user ratings and synopses, and search for your favorites anytime, anywhere.

Powered by The Movie Database (TMDb) API, our app ensures you have access to the latest and most comprehensive movie data in a sleek and user-friendly interface.

Key Features

  • Popular Movies: Browse trending movies sorted by popularity to keep up with what's hot.
  • Movie Search: Quickly search for any movie by title with instant results.
  • Detailed View: See ratings, posters, and concise overviews at a glance.
  • Interactive UI: Hover over a movie poster to reveal additional information instantly.

Technology & Design

The app is built using modern web standards including HTML5, CSS3 with custom properties for theming, and JavaScript ES6+ featuring asynchronous operations and dynamic DOM manipulation.

A clean and consistent UI influenced by contemporary design principles ensures easy navigation and accessibility, while thoughtful UX patterns enhance user engagement.
